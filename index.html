ℹ️ Setup Guide: Why do web alarms fail when closed? ▼

Modern mobile browsers (iOS Safari & Android Chrome) freeze all website scripts when a tab is closed or your phone is locked to preserve battery and memory.

How to get 100% reliable system alerts:

🌐 How to host & edit this file on GitHub ▼

You can easily run this as a real website on your phone and edit it directly on GitHub. Here's how:

1. Uploading the file:
  1. Open your repository on GitHub.com.
  2. Click Add file → Upload files.
  3. Drag and drop your index.html, manifest.json, sw.js, and icon.svg files and commit the changes.
2. Turning on Free Website Hosting (GitHub Pages):
  1. Go to your repository's Settings tab.
  2. On the left sidebar, click Pages.
  3. Under "Build and deployment" → "Source", select Deploy from a branch.
  4. Under "Branch", select main (or master) and click Save.
  5. In 1-2 minutes, GitHub will give you a live link like https://your-username.github.io/your-repo/ that you can bookmark on your phone!
3. How to edit text or details directly on GitHub:
  1. Open your repository on GitHub.
  2. Press the . (period) key on your keyboard. This opens an online code editor (VS Code in browser!).
  3. Search for names or times (e.g., search for Womiron or scan dates). Make your edits directly.
  4. Go to the Source Control tab on the left bar (looks like a branch icon), type a commit message, and click Commit and Push. Your website will update automatically in 1 minute!
4. How to sync Google Calendar on Android (both phones):
  1. Share the Calendar: Go to Google Calendar on a desktop, hover over your "Mouni & Baby Health Hub" calendar, click the three dots → "Settings and sharing". Add your wife's Gmail email.
  2. Accept the Share: Your wife must check her Gmail on her phone or computer and click the link inside to add the calendar.
  3. Sync on Android App:
    • Open the Google Calendar app on both your Android phones.
    • Tap the menu button (top-left, three lines) → scroll down and tap Settings.
    • Tap the newly added "Mouni & Baby Health Hub" calendar. (If you don't see it, tap "Show more").
    • Toggle "Sync" to **ON** (very important, otherwise it won't download updates!).
    • Ensure Notifications are set to make sound alerts.
